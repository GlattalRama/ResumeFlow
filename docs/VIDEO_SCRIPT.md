# ResumeFlow — Walkthrough Video Script

A scene-by-scene script for a **~4–5 minute** end-user (job seeker) walkthrough. Derived from [`USER_MANUAL.md`](./USER_MANUAL.md).

## How to use this script
- **NARRATION** = paste straight into an AI voiceover tool (ElevenLabs, Murf, PlayHT) or read aloud.
- **ON SCREEN** = what to record/show during that line (screen capture of the real app).
- **B-ROLL / TEXT** = optional captions or zoom callouts to add in editing (Descript, CapCut).
- Keep recordings at the real app's pace; trim dead time in editing.

**Suggested production pipeline:** record the real screens (Loom/OBS) → generate narration from this script (ElevenLabs) → assemble + caption (Descript). Total runtime target: 4–5 min.

**Recommended voice/tone:** friendly, confident, unhurried. ~150 words/min.

**Before recording, prepare a demo account with:** one complete resume set as the Base Resume, and one application that has the **job description pasted as text**.

---

## Scene 0 — Cold open / title (0:00–0:12)

**ON SCREEN:** App logo / dashboard, clean.
**TEXT:** "ResumeFlow — tailor your resume to every job."

**NARRATION:**
> "Applying to lots of jobs? ResumeFlow helps you build one great resume, then tailor it to each job with AI — and track every application in one place. Let's take a quick tour."

---

## Scene 1 — Sign in & dashboard (0:12–0:35)

**ON SCREEN:** Click **Sign in** → Google sign-in → land on **Dashboard**. Pan across the top nav.

**NARRATION:**
> "First, sign in with your Google account. ResumeFlow privately stores everything in your own Google Drive — your resume content is never used for analytics. Once you're in, you'll see your dashboard, with Resumes, Applications, and Settings up top."

---

## Scene 2 — Create a resume (the fast way) (0:35–1:15)

**ON SCREEN:** **Resumes → + New resume**. Click **Import from PDF/Word**, upload a file, show fields auto-filling. Scroll the populated form.

**TEXT callout:** "AI import — review the results!"

**NARRATION:**
> "Let's build a resume. The fastest way is to import an existing one — click 'Import from PDF or Word,' upload your file, and the AI reads it and fills in your details automatically. Always give it a quick review, since AI extraction isn't perfect. Prefer to start fresh? Just fill in the cards — basics, summary, experience, skills, and more — and watch the live preview update as you type."

---

## Scene 3 — Improve with AI & sections (1:15–1:50)

**ON SCREEN:** In the **Summary** card, click **Improve with AI**; show the text sharpen. Then open **Document Sections** — drag to reorder, rename a heading, toggle visibility.

**NARRATION:**
> "See these 'Improve with AI' buttons? They rephrase your text to read more clearly — without inventing anything. And in 'Document Sections,' you can reorder sections, rename a heading, hide what you don't need, even add a page break. Whatever you set here flows through to your preview and every download."

---

## Scene 4 — Templates & styling (1:50–2:25)

**ON SCREEN:** Open **Template** card, switch between templates (show **Cognizant Corporate** with its logo + projects page). Open **Font & Colors**, change accent color/font. Toggle **ATS-safe mode** on the preview.

**TEXT callout:** "ATS-safe = single column, no photo — best for online portals."

**NARRATION:**
> "Pick a template that fits — like the branded Cognizant Corporate layout. Adjust fonts, colors, and margins under 'Font and Colors.' And when you're submitting through a company's online system, flip on 'ATS-safe mode' — it switches to a clean, single-column layout that automated screeners read reliably."

---

## Scene 5 — The Base Resume (2:25–2:55)

**ON SCREEN:** On the Resumes list, click **Set as base**; show the green **★ Base Resume** badge. Briefly show the delete prompt behavior.

**TEXT callout:** "Base Resume = your master copy (can't be deleted)."

**NARRATION:**
> "Now mark your best version as the Base Resume — think of it as your master copy. It's the default starting point for tailoring, and it's protected: you can't delete your Base Resume, so you'll never lose your master by accident. To remove it, just set a different resume as the base first."

---

## Scene 6 — Track applications (2:55–3:25)

**ON SCREEN:** **Applications → + New application**. Fill company, role, and **paste the job description as text** (emphasize). Save. Show the **Status** pipeline and Notes.

**TEXT callout (important):** "Paste the job description as TEXT — not a screenshot."

**NARRATION:**
> "For every job you're chasing, add an application — company, role, and the job description. One important tip: paste the job description as text, not a screenshot, because that's what powers the AI features. Then move each job through your pipeline — Applied, Interview, Offer — and keep notes as you go."

---

## Scene 7 — Tailor the resume to the job (3:25–4:15)

**ON SCREEN:** On the application, click **Tailor Resume for this Job** → choose source (Base Resume) → **Generate** → show the "what changed" list (rephrased / reordered / emphasized) → **Accept & save as new version** → land on the new tailored resume.

**TEXT callout:** "Tailoring never invents facts — it only rephrases & reorders what's already there."

**NARRATION:**
> "Here's the magic. On the application, click 'Tailor Resume for this Job.' Pick your Base Resume and hit Generate. The AI rewrites and reorders your existing content to match this job — and it never invents skills or numbers you don't have. Review what changed, then save it as a brand-new version. Your master stays untouched, and now you've got a resume aimed right at this role."

> *(If you have no resume yet, ResumeFlow will simply prompt you to create one first — tailoring always works from an existing resume.)*

---

## Scene 8 — Interview prep (4:15–4:40)

**ON SCREEN:** Open **Interview preparation** → click **Generate Interview Q&A**, then show **Cover Letter** / **Follow-up** options.

**NARRATION:**
> "When you land the interview, open 'Interview preparation.' With one click, generate likely interview questions and answers, a prep briefing, a cover letter, or a follow-up message — all drawn from your resume and this specific job."

---

## Scene 9 — Export & close (4:40–5:00)

**ON SCREEN:** On a resume, click **Download PDF** (show the clean output). Quickly show DOCX/PPTX buttons. End on the logo/title card.

**TEXT:** "Build once. Tailor everywhere."

**NARRATION:**
> "Finally, download your resume as PDF, Word, or PowerPoint — ready to send. That's ResumeFlow: build one strong resume, tailor it to every job, and stay on top of your search. Give it a try!"
